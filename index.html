Discover the 7 Wonders

The 7 wonders of the ancient world was compiled more than 2000 years ago by Hellenic travellers. In 2001 the Swiss company New7Wonders Foundation launched a worldwide project to select the new 7 wonders as unfortunately only one of the original 7 still stand today. This is the pyramids of Giza. Each of the 7 wonders are also UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
